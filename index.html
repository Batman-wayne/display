About

Hello! I'm Harris, and I'm currently a senior at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research and projects primarly deal with hardware acceleration, AI, and computer vision. When I'm not working on these projects, I like to create cool stuff in Blender and watch thrillers like Mindhunter.
